About Us

Couple words about our Company


Created in 2010, SpotiSell has become one of Ireland's most successful car dealership groups in recent years, having secured up to 20 dealerships with other car manufacturers like Nissan, BMW, Volkswagen and Toyota to name a few. SpotiSell currently operates these dealerships located in Dublin, Dundalk, Galway and Belfast.


Amidst our outlets and production line, we deal in selling cars old and new and provide full vehicle maintainance facilities via state of the art workshops manned by fully qualified and experienced technicians.


On top of this, SpotiSell offer extensive full-on repair facilities with approved repairers from insurance companies such as Zurich and FBD.
